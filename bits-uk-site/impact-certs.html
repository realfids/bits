Market mechanisms for R&D capital allocation вҖ” using conditional forecasting to price, trade, and fund breakthrough research

The Problem

Philanthropic funding has terrible information properties

Traditional grantmaking forces a single entity to simultaneously predict which projects will succeed, evaluate whether they did, and provide the capital. Markets separate these roles.

Three roles conflated in grantmaking:
Doers вҖ” people who execute projects
Evaluators вҖ” people who assess impact
Capital allocators вҖ” people who bear risk

Certificates of impact (Christiano, 2014) proposed separating these roles by creating tradeable claims on completed work.
